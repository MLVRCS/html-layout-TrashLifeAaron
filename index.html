Best My Hero Academia Fights

  • London

    • Deku vs Class 1-A

    Not wanting to confront his classmates, Deku opts to run away, ignoring the taunts from Dynamight about how far he's fallen. He thanks everyone for coming, before fleeing from his friends using En's Smokescreen Quirk. However, Class A are knowledgeable about most of the Quirks that Deku had inherited and therefore know what steps to take to neutralize some of Deku's abilities. Dynamight shouts to the rest of the class not to let him get away, utilizing his new Ultimate Move, Land Mine Blast, to dissipate the surrounding smoke, and teases Deku for underestimating them.
